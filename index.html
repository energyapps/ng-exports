U.S. Natural Gas Export Countries

The United States became a net natural gas exporter on an annual basis in 2017 for the first time in almost 60 years. The transition to net exporter occurred as natural gas production in the United States continued to grow, reducing pipeline imports from Canada and increasing exports, both by pipeline and as liquefied natural gas (LNG).
